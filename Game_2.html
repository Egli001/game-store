Name: historical moment

Release date: 11/9/2001

Reating: 3/5

Discription:

Price: 50 Sek

In "Historical Moment," you take on the role of an air traffic controller tasked with preventing a disaster. Guide planes safely through the skies, make quick decisions, and use your skills to stop a plane from crashing into a building. Your actions can change history—can you keep everyone safe?
